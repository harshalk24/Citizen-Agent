The Product

Five screens. Complete citizen journey.

Everything a citizen needs from their government — from discovery to action — in one place.

🎯
My Situation — Entitlement Discovery
3 taps: pick your life event, employment status, and country. The AI instantly surfaces every government benefit, grant, and scheme you qualify for — with amounts, deadlines, and documents needed. No forms. No jargon.
Example: Amara just lost her job in Dublin. She taps "Lost my job" → Ireland → the system surfaces Jobseeker's Benefit (€232/week), Back to Education Allowance, and 4 other schemes — all in under 3 seconds. Total potential value: €8,400/year.
📋
Action Plan — Week-by-Week Roadmap
The AI generates a prioritised action plan: what to apply for this week, what can wait, what documents to gather. Each step is sequenced to maximise approval chances and avoid missed deadlines.
Example: Rohan in Mumbai just had a baby. His plan says: Week 1 — register the birth, apply for Maternity Benefit. Week 2 — claim Child Care Subsidy. Month 3 — apply for PM MATRU VANDANA. Total unclaimed value identified: ₹35,000.
🤖
AI Agent Chat — Your Personal Government Guide
A conversational AI that knows your situation, understands your country's benefit system, and answers specific questions — not just generic information. It links directly to the schemes relevant to you and explains eligibility in plain language.
Example: Sarah asks "Will I lose my Jobseeker's if I do a part-time course?" The agent explains the Back to Education rules for Ireland, tells her exactly which form to submit, and links directly to the Department of Social Protection portal.
📊
Dashboard — Citizen Control Centre
A personal dashboard tracking every entitlement, its status (not started / in progress / completed), upcoming deadlines, and a summary of the action plan. Citizens see their full benefit picture at a glance.
Example: Fatima checks her dashboard 3 weeks after starting a business in the UAE. She sees: 2 schemes completed ✓, 3 in progress, 1 deadline in 8 days (trade licence renewal). She clicks "Ask agent" on the renewal and gets exact steps in 10 seconds.
👤
My Profile — Richer Context, Better Results
Citizens can enrich their profile with salary range, housing status, family details, and goals. When saved, this automatically re-runs the entitlement check — surfacing schemes they wouldn't have found with just the 3-tap flow.
Example: David adds "2 dependents" and "Renting" to his Ireland profile after job loss. The system immediately re-runs and surfaces Rent Tax Credit and Child Benefit on top of his existing results — €1,800/year he didn't know about.

Where We're Going

From reactive tool → proactive agent

The MVP proves the core: citizens find benefits they didn't know existed. The next evolution flips the model — the agent reaches out to the citizen, not the other way around.

Tomorrow — Proactive Agent
→ Agent detects life events from signals
→ Pushes "You may now qualify for X" alerts
→ Prepares application drafts automatically
→ Tracks deadlines and nudges citizen
→ Closes the loop — "Your appeal is due"
The proactive agent vision in one sentence:
"You shouldn't have to know a benefit exists to receive it — the agent should tell you the day you qualify, prepare everything you need, and remind you until it's claimed."
Signal 1 New baby registered → agent immediately queues Maternity Benefit, Child Benefit, Infant Formula Scheme without the parent asking.
Signal 2 Employment record shows termination → agent sends "You're likely eligible for Jobseeker's Benefit — here's your pre-filled application."
Signal 3 Annual renewal approaching → agent nudges 3 weeks before, not the day it lapses.
